Admission Process

PBS-PGDM- Admission Process:

  1. Admission to PGDM course shall be made only from the candidates qualified from any one of the six all India tests, ie: CAT/XAT/MAT/CMAT/ATMA/GMAT with minimum 50 percentile or the common entrance examinations (if any) conducted by the respective state governments.
  2. The selection shall be based on overall rank computed taking into account further components with the following weights.
    • Score in the Common Admission test 35-60%.
    • Score in academic performance of X,XII standards, with clear consistency and UG performance with the weight of 5 to 25%.
    • GD/PI- 20 to 45%.
    • Extra-curricular, sports, social work, Academic diversity, Gender Diversity etc with 5 to 20%.
